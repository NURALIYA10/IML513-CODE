Frequently Asked Questions

What is crochet?

Crochet is a technique of creating fabric from yarn using a hooked needle.

Do I need experience to join the class?

No experience needed. We welcome complete beginners!

What tools do I need?

You’ll need yarn and a crochet hook. Details are shared in the Orientation section.
