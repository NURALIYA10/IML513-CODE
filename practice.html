Daisy Coaster Crochet

Step 1
Do 3CH and 12HDC into the first ch. Sl St to join the round
Step 2
Then 2CH and 2HDC into each stitch, Sl st to join the round
Step 3
Skip 1 stitch, 3DC, skip 1 stitch and Sl St. Repeat the steps about 6 times.
Step 4
Next, 1DC, 3DC, 1DC and Sl st. Repeat the steps about 6 times.
Step 5
Lastly, 1DC, 2DC, 4DC, 2CD, 1DC and Sl st. Repeat the steps about 6 times
