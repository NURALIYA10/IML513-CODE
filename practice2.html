Practice 2

Crochet Scrunchie

Step 1
Start with a slip knot and chain 12-15 or long enough to wrap around your scrunchie
Step 2
Crochet half double crochet (hdc) into each chain. This will create a long strip
Step 3
Turn your work and repeat idc rows until the piece is wide enough to wrap around your hair elastic
Step 4
Place your crochet piece around a regular hair tie, then fold it in half to hide the hair tie inside
Step 5
Use a yarn needle to sew the edges closed. This will keep the scrunchie shape and hold everything in place
