Orientation & Tools

Crochet Hook

A crochet hook is a handheld tool used to make loops and pull yarn through them to create stitches. Hooks come in various sizes (measured in mm), and choosing the right size depends on the thickness of the yarn and the type of project. Common materials include metal, plastic, wood, or bamboo.

Yarn

Yarn is the main material used in crochet. It is a long strand of fiber that comes in different weights (thicknesses), textures, and colors. Beginners usually start with medium-weight yarn (also called worsted weight) because it's easier to see stitches and manage tension.

Yarn Snips

Yarn snips are small, sharp scissors specifically used for cutting yarn. They provide a clean, quick cut and are often more precise than regular scissors. Having a good pair of yarn snips is important for trimming ends and finishing off crochet projects neatly.

Stitch Marker

A stitch marker is a small clip or ring used to mark specific points in your crochet work. They are especially helpful for keeping track of the beginning of a round, pattern repeats, or stitch counts. Stitch markers can help prevent mistakes by giving you visual cues while you crochet.
